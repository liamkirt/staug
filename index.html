A Life of Public Service

The Honourable Khadijah Ameen is the Member of Parliament for St. Augustine and Minister of Rural Development and Local Government. First entering Parliament in 2015, she has served as an Opposition Senator, Opposition Member of Parliament, and now as a Cabinet Minister.

Her journey in public life began at a young age. Nominated as a Local Government Councillor at just 21 and elected at 22, she made history in 2010 as the youngest Chairman of the Tunapuna Piarco Regional Corporation — a milestone that reflects her early commitment to community and governance.

"Public service is not a privilege to be held — it is a responsibility to be lived."

She holds an MBA in Leadership from Anglia Ruskin University and is currently pursuing an LLB with the University of London, combining academic rigour with hands-on legislative experience.
